Get more information about a specific location

The map is interactive. You can get more information on a flood zone or surface water area on the map using a:

  • mouse: click on a point to find out more about the flood data held on that location
  • keyboard: navigate to the point, centre the crosshair on the location, then press enter
  • smartphone or touchscreen device: tap on a point on the map

To order detailed flood risk information for a specific location or site, you will need to draw a site boundary on the map.

Draw a boundary

You can use a polygon to draw a more detailed site boundary or use a square for a simple boundary. Choose what type of boundary you want to draw under ‘Get a boundary report’ on the navigation panel on the map page.

Using a polygon

  • click ‘Add polygon’
  • click or tap the screen to place the first point (or node) of your boundary
  • do not try to drag or swipe to draw a line, as that will move the map
  • click or tap the screen again to place the second point of your boundary
  • you should see a blue line drawn between the points
  • keep adding points until you have finished the boundary of your site
  • to finish your boundary, double click or double tap the screen
  • the boundary is finished when the lines turn red
  • click the ‘Finish’ button to set your boundary
  • click ‘Get summary report’ to get more detailed flood information

Using a square

  • click ‘Add square’
  • zoom and move the map to position the square
  • click the ‘Finish’ button to set your boundary
  • click ‘Get summary report’ to get more detailed flood information

Edit or delete a boundary

You can edit or delete your boundary by clicking ‘Edit shape’ or ‘Delete shape’ under ‘Get a boundary report’ on the navigation panel.
