Projected chance of flooding between 2096 and 2125

With defences (defended)

Between 2096 and 2125, taking flood defences into account, there is more than a 3.3% of a flood at this location in any year. 

What does ‘defended’ mean?
  • it assumes existing flood defences will work as intended
  • it does not make any assumptions about future maintenance or improvement
  • be aware that flood defences can fail or their design standards exceeded

Without defences (undefended)

Between 2096 and 2125, without flood defences, there is a 1% chance of a flood at this location in any year.

What does ‘undefended’ mean?
  • it ignores the benefits of any existing defences
  • it allows for flood defences where they could increase flood extents
