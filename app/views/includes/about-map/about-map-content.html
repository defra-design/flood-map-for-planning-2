Data included in the map

This service now includes data from the new national flood risk assessment (NaFRA2). See updates to national flood and coastal erosion risk information. 


Existing data

Unchanged:

  • main river lines
  • flood defences
  • water storage areas

Updated:

  • flood zones 2 and 3

New data

Rivers and the sea

Rivers and the sea extents with defences for present day and with climate change:

  • 3.3%
  • 1% fluvial and 0.5% tidal
  • 0.1% AEP

Rivers and the sea extents without defences for present day and with climate change:

  • 3.3%
  • 1% fluvial and 0.5% tidal
  • 0.1% AEP

Surface water

Surface water flood risk extents for present day:

  • 3.3%
  • 1%
  • 0.1%

Climate change

  • the allowances used have been chosen from the Environment Agency’s Flood risk assessment: climate change allowances
  • for river flooding we have used the ‘central’ allowance – based on the 50th percentile for the 2080s epoch
  • for sea and tidal flooding we have used the ‘upper end’ allowance – based on the 95th percentile for 2125
